Description

Brakin's Tavern was written in 48 hours as part of BGS Jam XIV at the Buffalo Game Spaces. It was a collaboration between Brian Cheverie, Kim Sucher, Matt Christensen and Victoria Christensen.

The theme was "Breaking the Law." Matt came to the jam wanting build something using Adventure Creator. In the initial brainstorming, someone suggested "a wizard running a speakeasy". This morphed fairly quickly into "standard fantasy bar".

All art, voiceovers, music, and sound effects are original and created durning the jam.

Features

  • Drag and drop gameplay
  • Original music
  • Original art
  • Will only take 5 minutes of your time!
